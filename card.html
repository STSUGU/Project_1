Arabian oryx


The Arabian oryx is amongst the largest desert mammals of the Arab region and throughout history has been an important aspect of its heritage.

  • Life span:
    20 years
  • Weight:
    65-70kg
  • Height:
    81-120cm
  • Lenght:
    s160cm

Its bright white coat serves to reflect the sun’s rays and its hooves are shovel-like and splayed, giving the animal a large surface area when it walks on the sandy ground. Both males and females have splendid straight, ringed horns as long as 68 centimeters (27 in) long; the female’s being thinner and longer than those of a male. These animals are famous for their stamina and beauty and have inspired many Arabian poets. source:ANIMALIA
